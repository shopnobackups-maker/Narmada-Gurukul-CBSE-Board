gbrilliance

A unique foundation developed and maintained by gurukul for the comitative gurukulites

  • Competitive exam course
  • Fortnightly online Exams
  • Regular Assesment and mentoring

GBrilliance, crafted by Gurukul, is a distinct foundation program aimed at students from class 5 and above, preparing them for competitive exams like JEE and NEET. Through specialized study materials and biweekly online exams, it equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their academic and professional futures, embodying Gurukuls commitment to holistic education and excellence.

gstatus

Program for overall Academic, discipline, Punctuality, Cleanliness, Sanskaar, etc assessment of your child.

  • Self Assesments
  • Daily Monitoring
  • gStar targets

gStatus is a unique, comprehensive assessment program by Gurukul, evaluating students in academics, discipline, punctuality, cleanliness, adherence to dress code, sports prowess, and overall behaviour, aiming for their all-round development. Recognized achievers receive accolades, including certificates and trophies, in a ceremonial acknowledgement by special guests and Swamiji, motivating excellence and growth.
