10 Years Experience

With a rich legacy spanning over 75 years, Shree Swaminarayan Gurukul International School continues to set the standard for educational excellence in India. Since its inception in 1948, the school has remained steadfast in its mission to nurture well-rounded individuals through the principles of Vidya, Sadvidya, and Brahmavidya, thereby contributing positively to society. With state-of-the-art campuses strategically located in key megacities such as Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Nagpur, Raipur, Delhi, Ahmedabad, and beyond, the school provides students with unparalleled opportunities for holistic growth and development.
