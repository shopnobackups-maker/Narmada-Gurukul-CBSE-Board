Discover Holistic and Value-Based Education for Your Child

Looking for a school in Bharuch- Ankleshwar that focuses on both academic excellence and character development? Shree Swaminarayan Gurukul offers a balanced education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit.

Spread across a serene 10-acre campus, the school provides a peaceful and disciplined environment that encourages learning, self-discovery, and personal growth. Our educational philosophy integrates modern academic practices with the timeless values of the Gurukul tradition.

The curriculum is structured around:

  • Vidya – Academic knowledge and intellectual growth
  • Sadvidya – Moral values, discipline, and right conduct
  • Brahmavidya – Inner awareness and spiritual understanding

This balanced approach enables students to excel academically while developing ethical values, social responsibility, and emotional maturity.

Students interact with fostering cultural awareness, confidence, and global understanding. Guided by experienced and dedicated educators, every child is supported to discover their strengths and achieve their highest potential.

Hostel Facilitates

At our residential campus, we provide a safe, nurturing, and home-like environment where children receive round-the-clock care, guidance, and emotional support. Thus making them feel at home.

Residences

Dormitory

Dormitories are equipped with individual beds, personal cupboards, chairs, and footwear racks, ensuring comfort and personal space.

Mineral Water Plant

Recognising the importance of safe drinking water for a child’s health, the campus is equipped with a central mineral water purification plant. Clean and purified drinking water is supplied across the campus, along with water coolers for chilled water.

Solar Water Heating System

To meet students’ daily needs in all seasons, the campus has an eco-friendly solar water heating system. Hot water is supplied through separate pipelines using multiple solar heater collectors, ensuring safety, sustainability, and comfort.

Services

1. Gurukul Store

The Gurukul Store is a well-organised campus facility that meets students’ daily needs. It is fully stocked with essential stationery such as pens, pencils, notebooks, and textbooks. For transparency and discipline, each student’s purchases are managed through a monitored pocket-money account, which is regularly updated and supervised by the school administration.

2. Student’s Communication Facility

Students are provided with a dedicated communication wing where students can regularly connect with their parents and relatives, ensuring they feel supported and motivated while away from home.

3. Laundry Services

To promote hygiene and convenience, Students' uniforms and linens are cleaned 5 days a week and maintained with the highest standards of care, allowing them to focus entirely on their academic and co-curricular pursuits.

4.Salon Services

Maintaining a neat and disciplined appearance is a key aspect of the Gurukul lifestyle. Our grooming services ensure that students adhere to the school’s dress code and personal hygiene standards.

5. Tailor Services

To ensure a perfect fit and professional appearance, we provide on-site tailoring services for school uniforms and ethnic wear, catering to the individual requirements of every student.
