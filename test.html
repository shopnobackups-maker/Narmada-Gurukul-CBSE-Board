Discover Holistic and Value-Based Education for Your Child Today

Are you looking for the best school in Bangalore? Look no further! We offer a unique combination of holistic learning that enables the mind and spirit to develop at our residential campus. Our campus spans about 17 acres, providing students with a peaceful environment to explore and grow. We believe that education can transform lives by integrating modern holistic education schools in Bangalore approaches within the framework of our Gurukul Value System. Our curriculum emphasizes Vidya, Sadvidya, and Brahmavidya, guaranteeing that learners excel academically while remembering to be thoughtful, ethical, and socially conscious. Your kid will have the chance to engage with counterparts from 23 states and 7 countries, helping them develop as well-rounded people. Our devoted instructors are committed to providing every student with the awareness they need to reach their highest possibility.

Mineral Water Plant

Drinking water matters much in a child’s health. SGIS is set with a Mineral Water Plant, that refines the drinking water, throughout the campus. With this, water coolers are also kept to provide chilled water to Students.

Solar Plant

A child needs water in different temperatures in various seasons for bathing. Therefore, Gurukul provides separate pipelines, coming from around 35 Solar Heater Collectors, for hot water.

Ethics & Spirituality

Pooja

Every morning at 05:30 AM, Students come to prayer hall for pooja. It includes, chanting of Swaminarayan Mahamantra, Recitation of Devotional Songs, etc. One can feel Paradise at Gurukul during this hour.

Evening Prayer

All the holy saints conduct evening prayer every day with a focus on strengthening values.

Brahmavidya Sessions

This is special session once every week. In this session students learn new topic of spirituality.

Spiritual Prayers

Spiritual Prayers take place once every week, in this session, students learn scriptures and other religious activities.
